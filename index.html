Who We Are

Built on a love for good food
and good people.

Nally's Kitchen is a family-owned Mexican restaurant in Davenport, created out of something simple: a love for homemade food at affordable prices, and a love for the people we feed.

We make our corn tortillas, salsas and sauces fresh every single day — that's the distinctive homemade flavor folks keep coming back for. Cooking with family recipes, time-honored techniques and the freshest ingredients, we've become a neighborhood favorite, called the best Mexican food in town again and again.

Ask anyone about the Chicken Over Rice — that secret spice blend is all ours.
